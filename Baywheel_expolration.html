Bay wheels is a regional public bicycle sharing system in California's San Francisco Bay Area. It is operated by Motivate in a partnership with the Metropolitan Transportation Commission and the Bay Area Air Quality Management District.Bay Wheels is the first regional and large-scale bicycle sharing system deployed in California and on the West Coast of the United States . This documnet explores the data of Bay wheels for year 2019. The dataset used for the analysis is the marge of Bay Wheels trip history data for each month in 2019 starting from january until December.
